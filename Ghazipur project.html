  1. Ghazipur District-

Ghazipur district is a district of Uttar Pradesh state in northern India.The city of Ghazipur is the district headquarters. The district is part of Varanasi Division.District Magistate of Ghagipur is Aryaka Akhori. It occupy area of around 3,377km2 .It's population is around 3,620,268million, In which male's are around 18,55,000 million and females are around 17,65,000 million. People of Ghazipur mainly used to speak Hindi but some use to speak Urdu. In Ghzipur there are around 3,385 villages like Zakhniya, Manihari, Sadat Saidpur, Devkali, Virnao, Mardah etc. In Ghazipur there are 6 colleges, 6 hospitals, 2 important government schools etc.

Fact-

Ghazipur is well known for its opium factory, established by the British East India Company in 1820 and still the biggest legal opium factory in the world, producing the drug for the global pharmaceutical industry.
